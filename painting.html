Prompts For Painting/Drawing

10 prompts to help you generate ideas!

How Do I Get Back Into Painting or Drawing?

Just like art block, starting with the basics of painting or drawing again can help you get back into the flow. Pick any object near you that is not too hard to draw or paint, and start sketching the simple shapes out of it. You can also try playing around with colours. Overtime, you'll find yourself back in the flow of drawing or painting! Take it easy, don't force yourself to be extremely good at painting or drawing.

Prompt List

Here are the list of prompts you can choose from to get started.

  1. Spin a wheel with random animals and draw the first one you get.
  2. Draw your favourite food.
  3. Draw a character with fantasy elements.
  4. Draw something related to the season you're currently in.
  5. Draw your favourite cartoon character.
  6. Draw something related to the month you're currently in.
  7. Draw a bouquet of your favourite flowers.
  8. Draw any fruit with a random colour scheme.
  9. Draw the pastries you'd sell in your dream bakery.
  10. Draw your favourite video game character.
