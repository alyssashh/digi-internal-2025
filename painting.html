How Do I Get Back Into Painting or Drawing?

Just like art block, starting with the basics of painting or drawing again can help you get back into the flow. Pick any object near you that is not too hard to draw or paint, and start sketching the simple shapes out of it. You can also try playing around with colours. Overtime, you'll find yourself back in the flow of drawing or painting! Take it easy, don't force yourself to be extremely good at painting or drawing again.
