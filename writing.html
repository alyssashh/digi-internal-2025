Prompts For Writing

10 prompts to help you generate ideas!

Writing Tips

  • I find it really helpful to create a brainstorm of the elements I want to include in my story. Once I've figured out what I want to include, I try relate them to each other to create a story.

  • Read! Reading always gives you a greater set of vocabulary. It can also give you ideas for your own story.

  • Plan well. Plan your characters well, think about their personalities and appearances, maybe even their backstory. Plan your setting well, think about what era you want your story to be set in. Plan your plot well, think about how you want your story to start, flow, and end.

    Prompt List

    Here are the list of prompts you can choose from to get started.

    1. Write about a place where you can send letters to the dead. Who would you send it to?
    2. Write a story where your characters find out they are in the story you wrote.
    3. Write a love story where the character's way of confessing is through a set number of letters. And in each letter, they draw a flower that means something.
    4. Write a story based off your favourite song.
    5. Write a story based off your favourite show/movie, but change it to how you wished it would end.
    6. Write a story about a person who can see ghosts, and they're on a mission to help them find peace before moving to the afterlife.
    7. Start your own journal!
    8. Describe colours like they're people.
    9. Write a letter to your younger self.
    10. Write a letter to your future self.
