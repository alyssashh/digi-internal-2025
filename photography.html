Prompts For Photography

Apps for photography

If you want to touch up your photos, here are some of the apps you can use:

  • Adobe Lightroom - My favourite app to use to edit photos!
  • Fomz - My favourite app to take photos with camera filters.
  • OldRoll - Has a wide range of camera filters you can use to take photos.
  • Buttercam - Another app with a range of vintage camera filters.

Prompt List

Here are the list of prompts you can choose from to get started.

  1. Make an album of photos that capture reflections in water.
  2. The present and past - Recreate old photos and compare them side by side. Is anything different?
  3. Make an album of photos that capture your favourite flowers. This can be like an exihibition of flowers.
  4. Take a photo of the sky from different hours of day.
  5. For two weeks, capture the sunset from wherever you are.
  6. Take photos that have the same colour scheme and make an album out of it.
  7. Make your own restaurant by taking pictures of food and making an album out of them!
  8. For a week or two, take photos that you think represent different emotions. Each day, choose a different emotion.
  9. Make a timeline of photos from different eras leading up to the present, or even the future you think it'd look like. Take photos that you think capture a specific time period. (For example, the Victorian Era).
  10. Pick a song you like. Create a moodboard for that song out of the pictures you took that fit the song's theme.

Other Photographers

These are some of my favourite photographers you can check out for further inspiration.

Na Jaemin

Image credit: licensed under Unsplash

His Work

As photography is one of his hobbies, he made a photo exhibition named "Narcissisim" out of the personal photos he took. The button takes you to a video showing the process of his exhibition.

Yang Yongliang

Image credit: licensed under Unsplash

His Works

He is a Chinese contemporary artist who blends photography, images, and traditional Shan Shui (Chinese Landscape Painting) to show tradition and modernity together. Some of his famous projects are "Artifical Wonderland", "Five Dragons", and many more. My personal favourite is "Glows in the Arctic".

Sara Wager

Image credit: licensed under Unsplash

Her Works

She is an astrophotographer who takes pictures of space. All her photos look phenomenal and magical.
